## 🚀 Features

- **Real-time Simulation**: 60fps smooth animation with personality-driven movement
- **Interactive Experience**: Click to add bees, click bees to see their details
- **Personality Visualization**: Each bee's appearance reflects their traits
- **Social Networks**: Watch friendships form and influence behavior
- **Achievement System**: Bees unlock achievements based on their actions
- **Cultural Development**: Colonies develop unique traditions and art styles
- **Scientific Accuracy**: Waggle dances based on real bee research

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Next.js 15** with App Router and Turbopack
- **TypeScript** for type safety and better development experience
- **Tailwind CSS** with custom bee-themed color palette
- **Framer Motion** for smooth, personality-driven animations
- **Custom Simulation Engine** with emergent behavior systems

## 🎮 How to Use

1. **Watch the Colony**: Observe bees as they explore, forage, build, and socialize
2. **Add New Bees**: Click anywhere on the canvas to add a bee with random personality
3. **Inspect Individuals**: Click on any bee to see their personality, friendships, and achievements
4. **Observe Patterns**: Watch how different personalities create unique colony cultures
5. **Control Simulation**: Use play/pause controls to study specific moments
